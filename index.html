What makes us different?

The market of algorithmic recommendations is a very saturated one. Every streaming platform has its own algorithms trying to serve up what they think you will like. And you know what, they all suck!

We Have No Incentives, No Biases

Streaming services will only ever recommend their own content and what is new. Infinimatch looks at a broad range of media available on all platforms or only the media available on your streaming services.

Content-Filtering Neural Network

Other algorithms use collaborative filtering, which looks at what other users like to gain context on what you like. People are much more unique and it's much too difficult to place each of us in a bucket. You may love horror movies but watch rom-coms with your partner on the weekends. Infinimatch will take your unique tastes into account and suggest exactly what you want.

Who are we?

Infinimatch was created as part of the Tech Liftoff program, hosted by the Institute for Innovation and Entrepreneurship at Mount Royal University in Calgary Alberta. Designed to solve the problem of poor media recommendations. Khalaf Elwadya, Kostya Kirikeza and Velibor Tunic founded Infinimatch to give movie fans a more accessible way to find new movies to love.
